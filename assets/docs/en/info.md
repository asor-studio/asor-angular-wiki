## <span id="info-privacy">🛡️ Privacy Policy</span>

This policy describes how **ASOR Studio (Application Stability & Organizational Research)** handles data related to users who consult this documentation.

### Data Collected

This wiki documentation is a static client-side application.
> **No personal data is collected**, no tracking cookies are used, and no requests are made to external servers for the purpose of collecting information.

### Local Data

To improve your user experience, the application exclusively uses the browser's **Session Storage** to store your preferences (for example, the selected language).
This data:
- 🔒 **Remains local**: resides exclusively in your browser.
- 🚫 **Never transmitted**: is never sent to any server.
- 🧹 **Ephemeral**: is automatically deleted as soon as you close your browsing session.

### Third-Party Libraries

For an optimal interface, the documentation may load visual resources (such as fonts and icons) from external CDNs, which remain subject to the *privacy policies* of their respective providers.
